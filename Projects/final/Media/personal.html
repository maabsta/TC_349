The Sort

Results

I am trying to make a nice personal site that highlights some of my achievements and goals. I want a landing pad where people can get a feel for who I am (and see I can actually create a site).

I was a lot closer to agreement with participants in this card sort. This may just be because it is a pretty simple layout as of now (less options, less variety).

Everyone across the board agreed on where the home page should go which makes sense, seeing as it is the most important page. Then there was a toss up between work and school and obvious subjectivity for the sorter. I see school as my main focus so I put more priority with it. Most of the participants felt that hobbies were of less importance which makes sense compared to the other options. Overall, a nice sort to help guide me.
